# Business Trend & Sales Analysis – Case Study

## Project Overview

The **Business Trend & Sales Analysis – Case Study** is an interactive **Power BI** dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, shipment trends, and business profitability using **50,000+ sales records**. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales trends, regional performance, shipment efficiency, and profitability through dynamic visualizations and KPI-driven reporting.

By leveraging **Power BI**, **Power Query**, and **DAX**, this project enables businesses to monitor operational performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed, data-driven decisions.

## Tech Stack

* Microsoft Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Power Query
* CSV Data Processing
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Visualization

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales trends and business performance.
* Monitor shipment performance across different regions.
* Track profitability and revenue growth.
* Support strategic business decisions through KPI-driven insights.

## Dashboard Features

* Sales Trend Analysis
* Profitability Analysis
* Shipment Performance Tracking
* Region-wise Sales Analysis
* Product Category Performance
* Monthly and Yearly Trend Analysis
* Interactive Filters, Slicers, and KPI Cards

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Total Orders
* Shipment Performance Rate
* Monthly Revenue Growth
* Region-wise Profitability

## Data Processing

The dataset was prepared using **Power Query** through the following steps:

* Cleaned and transformed raw CSV sales data.
* Removed duplicate and missing records.
* Standardized product categories and shipment information.
* Built an optimized data model for dashboard reporting.

## DAX Calculations Used

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin Percentage
* Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
* Shipment Performance Metrics
* Dynamic KPI Measures

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories consistently generated higher profit margins.
* Shipment delays negatively impacted customer satisfaction and overall profitability.
* Sales performance increased during seasonal business periods.
* A few regions contributed significantly to overall revenue and profit growth.
